Our Story

We all fell in love with nature in our youth. We traveled past mountains, seas, plains and plateaus, and at last met on the southeast coast of India. Sharing our travel experiences, we all stumbled upon the same issue we had come to realize through the course of our journeys; The plight of Indian farmers!. We buckled up to solve this problem sustainably, and Voila!.

What is the problem?

By 2030 irrigation water demand doubles, while supply reduces nearly by one fourth at the current usage rate.

42% of Indian farmland is in water distress and Indian farmers are facing acute water shortage.The low productivity in farmlands due to extensive chemical fertilizer usage and flooding.

Ensuring food security for the upcoming 500mn+ population in-country is nearly a nightmare!

Existing solutions:

Micro-irrigation methods like drip irrigation fail after a year and thereafter, have high maintenance costs, especially affecting small-medium scale farmers with only 2.5-3 acre landholding.

Groundwater is depleting at an alarming rate, many farmers in Andhra Pradesh and Tamil Nadu face the first-hand adverse effects of bore wells drying up.

Canal irrigation failed to irrigate upland farms leaving an opportunity to develop a fertigation process for nearly 50% of the cultivable land.

What we Do

We are developing a micro fertigation (fertilization & irrigation) system for all-terrain agriculture. A novel seaweed-based hydrogel induced with bioactive N, P, K, and natural plant hormones for controlled release nutrients.A novel seaweed-based hydrogel induced with bioactive N, P, K, and natural plant hormones for controlled release nutrients.

Seaweed based formulation degrades itself into the soil after the cropping period removes the risk of micro pollution caused by semi-synthetic agricultural hydrogels. Delivering custom fertilizers for the farmers based on their crop and soil nutrition and creating a database for storing their crop and soil health data.
